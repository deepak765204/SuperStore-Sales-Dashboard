# SuperStore Sales Dashboard with Forecasting

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Sales Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze business performance across multiple dimensions including sales, profit, customer segments, and shipping modes.

It also incorporates a **15-day sales forecast**, enabling future trend analysis and supporting data-driven decision-making.

## Business Objective

The objective of this project is to:

- Monitor overall sales and profit performance
- Identify top-performing categories and sub-categories
- Analyze regional and state-level sales distribution
- Understand customer purchasing behavior
- Forecast short-term future sales

## Tools & Technologies Used

- Power BI (Data Visualization & Dashboard)
- Excel (Dataset)
- SQL (Data Analysis & Querying)

## Dashboard Features

### 🔹 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- **Total Sales:** 1.56M
- **Total Profit:** 175K
- **Profit Margin:** 11.19%
- **Total Quantity Sold:** 22K
- **Total Orders:** 1.56K
- **Sales Growth** tracking
- **Average Delivery Time**

### 🔹 Sales Analysis

- Sales by Category (Office Supplies, Technology, Furniture)
- Sales by Sub-Category (Phones, Chairs, Binders, etc.)
- Monthly Sales and Profit Trends (Year-wise comparison)

### 🔹 Customer & Shipping Insights

- Sales by Segment (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)
- Sales by Payment Mode (COD, Online, Cards)
- Sales by Shipping Mode (Standard Class, First Class, etc.)

### 🔹 Geographical Analysis

- State-wise sales distribution using map visualization
- Top-performing states like California, New York, and Texas

### 🔹 Forecasting (Advanced Feature)

- Implemented a **15-day sales forecast**
- Helps in demand planning and inventory decisions
- Visual representation of predicted trends alongside historical data

## Key Insights

- California generates the highest sales revenue
- Office Supplies is the top-performing category
- Phones and Chairs lead in sub-category sales
- Standard Class is the most preferred shipping mode
- Sales show an overall upward trend with fluctuations
- Forecast indicates continued growth in the short term
