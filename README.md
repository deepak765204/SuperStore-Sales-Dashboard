# SuperStore-Sales-Dashboard
> 🚀 This project simulates a real-world business scenario where data is used to analyze performance and forecast future sales trends.
Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze sales performance, profit trends, and customer behavior across regions and product categories. Includes KPI tracking, dynamic filtering, and a 15-day sales forecasting model to support data-driven decision-making.

## Business Insights

- Sales performance is highest in the West region, particularly in California
- Office Supplies contributes the most to total sales
- Standard Class shipping is the most preferred delivery mode
- Certain sub-categories like Phones and Chairs drive major revenue
- Forecast indicates a steady increase in sales in the upcoming days
